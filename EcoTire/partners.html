Partner 01/03

BeamNG.tech

Company Info

Our collaboration with BeamNG GmbH, a pioneering company based in Bremen, Germany, brings advanced simulation technology to our work. BeamNG.tech, developed by BeamNG GmbH, is a powerful simulator featuring a custom-built physics engine, initially created for the gaming industry and now adapted for research. The .tech version provides essential tools for autonomous vehicle development, ADAS testing, and vehicle dynamics, equipped with high-precision sensors such as cameras, LiDAR, IMU, and ultrasonic, and extensive ground truth support. This versatile platform offers customizable driving dynamics, configurable vehicles, and 12 open-world environments, allowing for the creation of realistic traffic simulations. With its open-source BeamNGpy library, BeamNG.tech enables seamless automation and rapid development, making it an invaluable tool for innovative vehicle testing and education in transportation technology.

Using their simulation system

We leveraged BeamNG.tech in multiple aspects of our development, with a primary focus on testing and simulating vehicle dynamics, autonomous driving capabilities, and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). The platform’s realistic physics engine and extensive range of customizable vehicles allowed us to create detailed scenarios that helped us fine-tune our technologies in controlled environments. Using BeamNG.tech’s diverse sensor suite and open-world settings, we were able to validate our solutions across a variety of real-world conditions, from city streets to racetracks, ensuring safety and performance at every stage.

Advancing Innovation Together

Our collaboration with BeamNG GmbH goes beyond simulation—it's a strategic partnership grounded in our mutual dedication to advancing automotive innovation. With BeamNG.tech integrated into our research and development process, we have not only enhanced our testing capabilities but also reinforced our commitment to delivering safe, efficient, and sustainable vehicle technologies. This partnership enables us to meet the highest standards of quality and reliability, aligning with our vision to shape the future of transportation through excellence and cutting-edge technology. Together, we are setting new benchmarks in the automotive industry.
